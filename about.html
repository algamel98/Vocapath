About VocaPath

VocaPath is a free vocabulary learning platform designed to help learners master English through structured, level-based learning.

What We Offer

Our platform provides access to the Oxford 3000 word list, organized by CEFR levels (A1 to C1). Each word includes Arabic translations, definitions, and pronunciation support.

Our Approach

We believe in simple, effective learning. VocaPath focuses on essential vocabulary without unnecessary complexity, allowing learners to progress at their own pace.

Who We Serve

VocaPath is designed for Arabic-speaking learners of English at all levels, from beginners to advanced students.
